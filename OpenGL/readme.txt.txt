
* all referenced files are located in "glutdlls37beta" folder.
* please note that the {version} may differ on your system.

1. Paste glut.h in C:\Program Files (x86)\Microsoft Visual Studio\2019\Community\VC\Tools\MSVC\{14.28.29333}\include\GL.
 Create the GL folder if not present already. 
2. Paste glut.lib in C:\Program Files (x86)\Microsoft Visual Studio\2019\Community\VC\Tools\MSVC\{14.28.29333}\lib\x64.
   Paste glut32.lib in C:\Program Files (x86)\Microsoft Visual Studio\2019\Community\VC\Tools\MSVC\{14.28.29333}\lib\x86.
3. Paste glut.dll and glut32.dll in C:\Windows\SysWOW64.
   Also copy glut32.dll to C:\Windows\System32
